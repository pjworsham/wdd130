Whitewater Rafting

Explore our Outdoor Adventures

Chattoga River

Embark on an epic adventure at the Chattooga River, where the border between South Carolina and Georgia becomes the stage for an unforgettable experience. Whitewater rafting here is an absolute thrill, making it a must try adventure in the Southeast. Perfect for adventurers aged 8 and above. This journey promises a scenic and mildly wild ride suitable for both kids and adults. Immerse yourself in the stunning natural beauty of the riverbanks.

Pigeon River

Whitewater rafting along the Great Smoky Mountains National Park. Experience rafting on the Pigeon River, conveniently located near Gatlinburg. We offer a rafting experience for a variety of ages. The Scenic Float Trip is perfect for families with younger children looking for a calm ride. The Whitewater Rafting Trip is great for those seeking a thrilling adventure.

Nantahala River

Nantahala River Rafting is a family friendly adventure located on the edge of the Great Smoky Mountain National Park in Western North Carolina. A guided Nantahala rafting trip is a great way for families and beginners to experience the beautiful river.
