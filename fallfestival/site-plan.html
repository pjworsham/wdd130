Overview

Purpose

My purpose for this site is to provide an informative platform where people can easily discover the activities available at our annual fall event. On this website, we will showcase a diverse range of events and activities suitable for all ages, including Arts & Crafts vendors, pumpkin decorating, face painting and events.

Audience

We will be holding our annual fall festival at the Etowah River Park, where we will feature activities suitable for all ages and provide a platform for local vendors to showcase their Arts and Crafts.

Home page

2023 Fall Festival

Saturday and Sunday, October 21 & October 22

Etowah River Park, 600 Brown Industrial Parkway Canton, GA. 30114

Fall festival events are an exciting and a spirited part of the autumn season, offering a wide range of activities and adventures for people of all ages to enjoy. One of the beauties of living in the foothills of the North Georgia Mountains, we enjoy the harvest season as we welcome and celebrate the transition from summer to autumn with the changing colors of leaves and the crisp cool air.

Get ready to embark on a furry adventure like never before at our animal extravaganza! It's not just a petting zoo it is a roaring good time for the whole family! Our talented animals are ready to strut their stuff, showcasing their unique skills and charming personalities.

Don't miss out on other fun events we have planned just for your enjoyment! Like our Halloween costume parade, corn maze and other memorable and enjoyable adventures.

One such festival is the Etowah River Park Fall Festival, which invites you to join in the celebration of autumn. This vibrant event takes place on Saturday (9 am – 6 pm) and Sunday (9 am – 5 pm) from October 21 to October 22, 2023, at the picturesque Etowah River Park located at 600 Brown Industrial Parkway in Canton, GA 30114. The festival is open to all and requires a $7 donation per person aged 11 and up for admission. All proceeds raised during the festival go toward supporting local children in need.

the Etowah River Park Fall Festival, you can expect to find over 25,000 patrons, more than 150 arts crafts exhibitors, several concessionaires, and a host of engaging activities. The festival is a fantastic opportunity to immerse yourself in the spirit of fall and make memories with your friends and family. Fall into Fun at Our Festival!

Events

The Etowah River Park Fall Festival caters to families and kids with an engaging array of activities designed to bring joy and excitement to the youngest festival attendees. Children can enjoy a range of games and amusements that support the season and the spirit of fun.

Face painting is a popular attraction, allowing kids to choose from their favorite animals, superheroes, or spooky creatures.

Step into the realm of pumpkin Magic! Transform your pumpkin into anything your heart desires! From spooky specters to friendly goblins, your imagination is the only limit! And when you have finished your masterpiece, join one of our other enchanting adventures.

Hayrides provide an autumn adventure for everyone to enjoy, taking a scenic tour of the festival grounds, while you enjoy picking out your favorite scarecrows that have been created by our amazing local businesses.

Get ready to embark on a furry adventure like never before at our animal extravaganza! It's not just a petting zoo it is a roaring good time for the whole family! Our talented animals are ready to strut their stuff, showcasing their unique skills and charming personalities.

Don't miss out on other fun events we have planned just for your enjoyment! Like our Halloween costume parade, corn maze and other memorable and enjoyable adventures.

Vendors

Our local exhibitors have poured heart and soul into unique creations that you can explore with a wide range of offerings, including paintings photographs, wood crafts, pottery and glassware, furniture, floral arrangements and decorative accessories, baskets, adult and children’s clothing, doll clothes and toys, jewelry, candles, soap and more.

Every single vendor calls our community home, making this festival a vibrant showcase of local talent. Support our neighbors, celebrate their creativity, and take home a piece of your community's soul!

What sets this festival apart is its commitment to featuring only items that are crafted with care and creativity. Find the perfect accessory to complement your style.

Don't miss out on the celebration of creativity that only happens once a year! It's not just a festival, it's an adventure waiting for you!
